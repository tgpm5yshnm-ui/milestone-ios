Common questions

Most things are answered here
Is Milestone free to use?
Yes. The Net Worth Calculator is completely free, forever — no trial, no sign-up. Live crypto prices, the stock calculator, and the portfolio tracker are part of Milestone Premium.
What's included in Premium?
Premium unlocks live crypto prices for the top 250 coins, a real-time stock calculator, and the Portfolio tab where you can track every asset's current value against your target price. Plans: $4.99/month, $34.99/year, or $199 once for lifetime access.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Open the Premium paywall from any locked tab (Crypto, Stocks, or Portfolio) and tap "Restore Purchases" near the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple. Go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, select Milestone, and tap Cancel Subscription. Lifetime purchases never renew and don't need to be cancelled.
Where does the live price data come from?
Crypto prices come from CoinGecko. Stock prices come from Twelve Data. Both update live when you open the app or tap refresh — Milestone never stores or sells your financial data.
My data disappeared after updating the app. What happened?
Your sliders, portfolio, and settings are stored locally on your device and should persist across updates. If something looks off, try force-closing and reopening the app first — if the issue continues, send us a message below with your device model and iOS version.
