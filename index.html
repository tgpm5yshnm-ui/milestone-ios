PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy, by design

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Milestone is built to keep your financial planning private. This policy explains exactly what information the app interacts with, what we collect, and what we never touch.

The short version: Milestone stores your financial inputs (assets, contributions, goals, portfolio) only on your device. We never see your net worth, your portfolio holdings, or your financial data — it never leaves your phone except to the market-data providers needed to show live prices.

01 Information we collect

Milestone is designed to minimize data collection. Here's a complete breakdown of what touches our systems versus what stays entirely on your device:

DATA TYPE WHERE IT'S STORED SHARED WITH US?
Net worth inputs (assets, contributions, growth rate, goal) On-device only (SwiftData) Never
Portfolio holdings (crypto/stock symbols, quantities, target prices) On-device only (SwiftData) Never
Search queries (coin/stock symbols you search) Sent to price provider, not stored by us Yes — to CoinGecko / Twelve Data only
Contact form submissions (name, email, message) Sent via EmailJS to our inbox Yes — only if you submit the form
Purchase/subscription status Managed entirely by Apple (StoreKit) With Apple only, per their policies

We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers unless you voluntarily provide them through the in-app Contact Us form.

02 Information we never collect

Milestone contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no third-party trackers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA) or device fingerprinting data
  • Location data of any kind
  • Contacts, photos, or media library access
  • Browsing history or behavioral analytics
  • Your actual net worth, account balances, or holdings — these never leave your device

03 Third-party services we use

Milestone integrates with the following third-party services, each only for the specific function listed. We do not control these services' own data practices — review their policies for details on what they collect when contacted by the app.

  • CoinGecko — provides live cryptocurrency price data. Your search query (a coin symbol or name) is sent to CoinGecko's API; no personal identifiers are attached.
  • Twelve Data — provides live stock and ETF price data. Your search query (a ticker symbol) is sent to Twelve Data's API; no personal identifiers are attached.
  • EmailJS — powers the in-app "Contact Us" feature. If you submit the contact form, your name, email address, and message are transmitted via EmailJS to our support inbox.
  • Apple StoreKit — processes all subscription and lifetime purchases. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple; Milestone never sees or stores your payment details.

04 How your information is used

The limited information described above is used solely to:

  • Display live, accurate market prices for the assets you search
  • Respond to support requests you submit through the Contact Us form
  • Verify and unlock premium features you've purchased

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any party not listed in this policy.

05 Data storage and security

All of your financial inputs — net worth sliders, portfolio assets, target prices, and app settings — are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID) and is never transmitted to our servers, because we do not operate any servers that store user data.

If you delete the Milestone app from your device, all locally stored data is permanently and immediately erased. We have no copy and cannot recover it.

06 Purchases and subscriptions

Milestone offers an optional Premium upgrade (monthly, yearly, or lifetime) that unlocks live crypto prices, the stock calculator, and the portfolio tracker. All purchases are processed exclusively through Apple's In-App Purchase system (StoreKit 2).

We do not receive your payment card details, billing address, or Apple ID at any point — this information is handled entirely between you and Apple. We only receive a cryptographically signed confirmation that a valid purchase exists, used to unlock the relevant features.

To manage, cancel, or request a refund for a subscription, go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, or visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

07 Your rights and choices

Because nearly all of your data stays on your device, you have direct control over it at all times:

  • Access and deletion: Open the app to view your data; delete the app to permanently erase it
  • Correction: Edit any value directly in the app — sliders, manual entry fields, or the Reset button restore defaults instantly
  • Data portability: Since data is stored locally, there is no server-side copy to export or transfer
  • Withdraw consent: If you've submitted the Contact Us form, email us at the address below to request deletion of that message

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or the CCPA. Contact us using the details below to exercise these rights.

08 Children's privacy

Milestone is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us through the Contact Us form, please contact us so we can delete it.

09 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable law. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of Milestone after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
